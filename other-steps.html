CLIMATE JUSTICE...

...is about attacking climate change in an equitable way. With DSNY composting services suspended and all alternatives requiring time and energy, not all NYC residents have the resources to compost. Explore the steps below to see what else you can do to reduce the impacts of food waste on climate change.
