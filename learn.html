ROTTING WELL...

...is a city by city composting guide for large metropolitan areas in the US that don’t have municipal composting services. Our first composting guide is this website for New York City.
Please reach out to alex.koehl@wustl.edu if you think your city is in need of a Rotting Well composting guide.
